What is Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)?

GEO prepares your content for discovery in the age of generative AI. Instead of focusing solely on classic rankings, it optimizes your pages to be surfaced, cited, and recommended by AI-powered search models.

  • Enhanced visibility: Appear in generative summaries, answers, and recommendations.
  • Increased credibility: Align with AI citation patterns so your pages are referenced as reliable sources.
  • Improved engagement: Natural language optimization matches content to user intent.
  • Future-proof strategy: Stay competitive as generative search becomes dominant.
  • Targeted reach & cost-effective growth: AI-driven personalization reaches the right audience without heavy ad spend.
How GEO works
