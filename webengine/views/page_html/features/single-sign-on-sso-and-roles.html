Base role types

Start with roles everyone understands.

Content.One ships with a spectrum of preconfigured roles so you can launch quickly with the right guardrails. Each role maps to common responsibilities across your organization.

Owner

Full access across Content, Schema, Media, Code, Leads, Redirects, Reports, Apps, Settings, and Accounts.

Launch instances, add domains, invite users, manage teams, and create tokens.

Admin

Same access as Owner without the ability to delete other users.

Perfect for operational leads who manage day-to-day governance.

Developer

Code-first teams with access to build models, components, redirects, and integrations.

Includes Content, Schema, Media, Code, Leads, Redirects, Reports, Apps, Settings.

Developer Contributor

All Developer access without publish permissions.

Ideal for contractors or QA engineers contributing safely.

SEO

Focused on discoverability with access to Content, Media, Leads, Redirects, Reports, and Apps.

Empower growth teams to optimize without touching code.

Publisher

Content, Media, Leads, Reports, and Apps access with publish rights.

Perfect for regional marketing teams with go-live responsibility.

Contributor

Create and edit in Content, Media, and Apps without publishing rights.

Submit workflow requests to escalate approvals to publishers.

Access Admin

Specialized administrative view for managing identities and tokens.

Grant to security teams who oversee account-wide governance.

Custom and granular roles

Define permissions down to the field level.

Go beyond base roles with tailored permissions that align to your workflow. Content.One lets you configure roles for individual models, entries, or even specific fields so every teammate gets exactly what they need.

  • Custom role creation – Build roles from scratch or extend base roles.
  • Tailored permissions – Choose who can save, create, publish, or delete per content type.
  • Granular application – Apply roles to a single site, section, entry, or field.
  • Workflow governance – Prevent accidental changes by limiting access to exactly what matters.
Teams & multi-site management

Scale governance with reusable teams.

Organize users into teams that mirror your organization—marketers, developers, agencies, and more. Assign a role once and cascade it across instances so adding or removing members is effortless.

  • Create teams for recurring collaborators and contractors.
  • Apply a team to multiple instances with a single action.
  • Update membership once and sync everywhere automatically.
  • Accelerate onboarding by granting access in minutes, not days.
