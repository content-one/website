Supporting creators starts with effective management

The creator economy is projected to top $100B, bringing a wave of “micro-entrepreneurs” who expect timely resources. Wattpad’s original portal debuted in 2018; by 2022, it needed a flexible relaunch to serve millions of storytellers.

  • Creators rely on Wattpad for audience growth, monetization tools, and program updates.
  • Marketing needed a CMS that kept pace without engineering bottlenecks.
  • An expanded content strategy required fast iterations and global performance.

The challenge

Wattpad needed to evolve beyond its hard-coded portal so every update didn’t require developer intervention. The new experience had to onboard, educate, and retain writers while scaling internationally.

1

Hard-coded pages made content changes slow and dependent on engineering time.

2

Resources needed to be targeted by creator type and program stage.

3

Rapid growth meant the site required rock-solid performance and governance.

How they did it

Wattpad teamed up with JJ Bassette of Bassette Web Solutions to launch a new Creator Portal powered by Content.One’s WebEngine and Parsley templating framework.

  • Collaborative design process aligned to Wattpad’s creative direction and program goals.
  • Information architecture mirrored page layouts, making schemas intuitive for editors.
  • Lazy loading, responsive images, and Fastly CDN optimizations ensured rapid delivery.
  • Marketing teams now launch blogs, resources, and announcements independently.
