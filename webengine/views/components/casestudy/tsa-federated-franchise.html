From Legacy Complexity to Unified Strategy

Dozens of domains and aging infrastructure made it difficult for The Salvation Army to serve donors or communities online. Content.One offered a single, scalable foundation.

The challenge

  • Siloed systems and inconsistent branding across 50+ domains
  • Slow content updates with limited developer availability
  • Confusing user journeys for donors, volunteers, and service seekers

The approach

  • Centralize national and territorial sites under one platform
  • Provide reusable templates and shared data models
  • Introduce modern APIs and automation for rapid publishing
